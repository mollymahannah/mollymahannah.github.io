Nebraska Basketball's growing audience

Since Tim Miles came to coach Men's Basketball at Nebraska in 2012, thing have slowly changed for the team that had a small loyal fanbase. Due to numerous wins in the 2013-2014 season and a bid at the NCAA tournament, the Nebraska Men's Basketball team has reached a tipping point. Fans are lining up outside the new Pinnacle Bank Arena to watch the boys play in an atmosphere comparable to a Nebraska Football Gameday. Nearby, in the Railyard, fans gather to watch the big screen and drink a few beers. When Nebraska wins the cheers can be heard from the North Bottoms.
